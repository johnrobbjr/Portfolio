About Me

Sponges are my spirit animal. I will absorb whatever information comes my way: new methodologies, information about a field I’m not familiar with, or design critiques. Criticism is something I recieve thankfully and use it to improve upon my skills as a designer.

Fast-paced environments suit me best: to-do lists and efficient systems bring a smile to my face. This efficiency leaves room for creativity, allowing me to try new things, iterate, fail, and learn.

I take on leadership opportunities gladly and draw upon my past leadership experiences to command those efforts. I’ve always enjoyed teaching, and my goal is to help others learn about UX one day.

Besides all that jazz, I like to make/listen to music, take photos, wear colorful socks, code a little bit, travel when I can (Cape Town and Seville are my favorites so far), and run through the city.
