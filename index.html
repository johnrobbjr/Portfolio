About Me

Sponges are my spirit animal. I will absorb whatever information comes my way: new methodologies, information about a field I’m not familiar with, or design critiques. I will always take criticism thankfully and use it to improve upon my own skills as a designer.

I work best in fast-paced environments: to-do lists and efficient systems bring a smile to my face. This efficiency leaves room for creativity, allowing me to try new things, iterate, fail, and learn.

I take on leadership opportunities gladly and draw upon my past leadership experiences to command those efforts. I’ve always enjoyed teaching, and I hope one day to help others learn about UX.

Besides all that jazz, I travel when I can (Cape Town and Seville are my favorites), make/listen to music, take photos, code a little bit, and run through the city.
